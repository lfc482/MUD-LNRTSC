# Model checkpoints

This folder contains trained model parameter files for the leave-one-well-out cross-validation setting.

- `proposed_test_well_1_best.pth`: trained with wells 2-6 and tested on well 1
- `proposed_test_well_2_best.pth`: trained with wells 1, 3-6 and tested on well 2
- `proposed_test_well_3_best.pth`: trained with wells 1-2, 4-6 and tested on well 3
- `proposed_test_well_4_best.pth`: trained with wells 1-3, 5-6 and tested on well 4
- `proposed_test_well_5_best.pth`: trained with wells 1-4, 6 and tested on well 5
- `proposed_test_well_6_best.pth`: trained with wells 1-5 and tested on well 6

Raw field drilling data are not included due to confidentiality restrictions.
